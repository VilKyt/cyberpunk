# CORRUPTION MECHANICS (GM REFERENCE)

## Who Can See It
Only magic users with active arcane/divine sensitivity.
A character must either: have spell slots, have a feature
tied to magic (Divine Sense, Arcane Recovery, etc.), or
succeed on a DC 16 Arcana/Religion check when in proximity.

## What It Looks Like
Purple-black iridescence. Like oil on water but biological.
Seems to pulse faintly when observed. Doesn't react to the observer.

## Corruption Levels
| Level | Description | Where Found |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Trace | Barely visible, single pulse | General Night City population |
| Marked | Steady slow pulse, iris tint | Arasaka branded staff |
| Saturated | Constant glow, visible veins | Undercity, near server hubs |
| Consumed | Purple overtakes natural colour | Experimental subjects, failures |

## Interacting With It
- **Divine magic** (Paladin, Cleric): can partially disrupt Marked level.
  Target seizes, pulses, may break conditioning temporarily.
  Draws immediate attention if witnessed.
- **Arcane magic**: can detect and analyse. Cannot disrupt directly
  without the right knowledge (quest reward from the Archivist).
- **Druidic/nature magic**: corruption recoils slightly. Nature
  features don't function normally near Saturated zones.

## The Undercity
Elevated corruption throughout. NPCs here show behavioural
drift — not mind control, more like emotional flatness,
reduced self-preservation, tendency toward Arasaka loyalty
with no clear reason. They don't know. The party will notice.
