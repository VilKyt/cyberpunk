# ACT 2 — INTO THE CITY

## Overview

The party enters Night City for the first time. The Badlands gave them context — an old world bleeding into a new one, nomads, Arasaka's long reach. The city is something else entirely. Vertical, loud, saturated, and underneath all of it, the soul-drain hum the party can almost feel if they pay attention.

**Tone:** Sensory overload, paranoia, moments of unexpected warmth. The players are powerful and still completely lost, but now they're lost in a crowd.  
**Sessions estimated:** 3–4  
**Key location:** Watson district — old Night City, pre-corporate layer, the kind of place things get buried
