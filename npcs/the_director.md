# NPC — THE DIRECTOR (THE LICH)

## Public Face
Senior executive, Arasaka Corporation.
Exact title unknown. Appears in no public records older than 40 years
— a new identity every few decades.

## True Nature
The lich from Wildemount. Approximately 1,000+ years old.
Has refined his phylactery concept into the **Soulkiller** —
a continental soul-harvesting engine running on Arasaka's
server infrastructure. "Secure Your Soul" is the public face.

## Motivation
- Enslave, extract, or destroy all magic users
  (correctly believes only they can stop him)
- Complete the Soulkiller network
- Maintain soul-drain passive income across Night City's population

## The Corruption
His mark runs through Arasaka's senior staff — controlled,
refined version of the Wildemount corruption. Loyal. Invisible.
They don't know they're branded.

## On the Party
Currently: mildly inconvenienced. Order: collect, not kill.
Magic users are resources. Dead ones are waste.
This assessment will change.
