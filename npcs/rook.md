# NPC — ROOK

## Role
Nomad leader. First contact. Reluctant ally.

## First Impression
Calm, clipped, practical. Doesn't ask who the players are —
asks how long they were down there. Operating on her own agenda.
The only question that matters to her initially: are they corp assets?

## What She Knows
- Suspected something was sealed in the Quiet Mile
- The discharge confirmed a theory she's been carrying
- Heard "souls are clean" on the Arasaka comms — went very still
- Her grandmother's story connects to the key NPC thread

## What She Won't Say Yet
- Why she was scanning the Quiet Mile specifically
- What her grandmother actually told her
- Where she's heard the word "Convergence" before (intercepted corp transmission, six months ago)

## What She Gives in Scene 2
- Shelter and food, conditionally
- Her grandmother's journal reference — the woman who kept almost finding someone in the Badlands
- Confirmation she heard "souls are clean" — won't say what it means yet
- A burner comm (her contact number only)
- A hand-drawn map: drop point at the Night City fringe + unmarked second location (the Archivist signal, southeast)

## Key NPC Connections
- **Vera** — weapons specialist, has been with the clan twelve years. Rook trusts her completely. Knows the fuller story of why Mote had to leave Night City.
- **Mote** — camp medic. Rook told him not to lead with the cyberware installation when new people arrive. She watches how he interacts with the party.
